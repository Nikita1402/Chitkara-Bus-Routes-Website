BUS ETTIQUETES

  1. There is no hooting, clapping, singing, loud talking in the bus.
  2. No one plays loud music (Earphone music is permitted).
  3. No playing of cards.
  4. Ragging in any form is prohibited.
  5. No photography in the bus.
  6. Bus to stop only on authorized stops and to follow only permitted route. Bus route will be displayed in the bus.
  7. Seats and fittings in the bus are not to be damaged.
  8. Render all the help to the coordinators to maintain discipline in the bus, when ever there is threat from outsiders.
  9. All students board & deboard bus at stops as mentioned in their Bus Pass.
  10. Eve teasing and public display of affection in any form will not to be permitted.
